About

Vaidya Sanatan Mishra

Vaidya Sanatan Mishra is a fourth-generation classical Ayurvedic practitioner. He cures his patients while introducing them to the holistic aspect of Ayurveda. Furthermore, Vaidya Sanatan Mishra’s dedication has been instrumental in establishing Ayurveda, and his formulation to treat modern diseases has been life-changing.
With the motto of “Ayurved Sabse Pehle”, he is vigorously working towards fostering Ayurveda into the mainstream healthcare system. He is currently working on formulating his own medicines and using the ancient wisdom that runs in his DNA.
This determination has led him to win a series of accolades such as the Karmaveer Chakra Blue Medal, Jiwan Amrit Award and Ayurveda Youth Icon Award.
