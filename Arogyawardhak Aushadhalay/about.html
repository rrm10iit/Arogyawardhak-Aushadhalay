About

Vaidya Sanatan Mishra

Proud owner of Arograwardhak Aushadhalay. With a BAMS degree in Ayurvedic Medicine from S.R.N.N.M Government Ayurvedic College, he has established himself as a respected authority in the field. His noteworthy achievements include being awarded the Karamveer Chakra Blue medal and being recognized as a Youth Icon in Ayurveda by the Jivan Amrit Foundation. Vaidya Sanatan Mishra is also an accomplished author, having written books such as 'Malang' and 'Sasti Kitab,' both captivating story books. He actively shares his wisdom on Ayurveda and promotes Ayurvedic medicines through his engaging presence on Instagram and Facebook.
